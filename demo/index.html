SEVEN

Sustainable Energy Via Efficient Neural-routing

The Problem

Most AI products send every prompt to a large cloud model, even when the prompt is simple enough for a smaller model. SEVEN asks the routing question before inference.

The Solution

Classify the prompt, choose the smallest capable path, then report the estimated energy used and saved against a cloud-only baseline.

Local-class Route

Simple factual prompts go to a small free model: meta-llama/llama-3.2-3b-instruct:free.

Cloud-class Route

Complex prompts go to a newer free model: google/gemma-4-26b-a4b-it:free.
